# Smart-City-Analytics-DB
1.Project Title

Smart City Analytics Dashboard – Excel

2.Short Description / Purpose

An interactive Excel dashboard built to analyze traffic congestion, volume, speed, and travel time across major Bengaluru areas and roads. This project focuses on understanding how weather, roadworks, parking usage, signals, and public transport influence traffic patterns using Excel analytics and visualization.

3.Tech Stack
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
Power Query (data cleaning and transformation)
Formulas and functions
Slicers and interactive dashboards

4.Data Source
Dataset sourced from Kaggle

5.Highlights
Area-wise traffic and congestion comparison across Electronic City, Hebbal, Indiranagar, Jayanagar, Koramangala, M. G. Road, Whitefield, and **Yeshwanthpur
Road and intersection performance analysis including Silk Board Junction, Hebbal Flyover, Sarjapur Road, Trinity Circle, and **Sony World Junction
Weather conditions impact on traffic volume and travel time
Effect of road construction on congestion levels
Relationship between parking usage and traffic density
Public transport usage vs congestion comparison
Traffic signal status influence on traffic flow
Monthly traffic volume trend analysis
Interactive slicers for detailed analysis


6.Key Questions Addressed
Which areas in Bengaluru experience the highest traffic congestion and volume?
Which roads and intersections are the most traffic-prone?
How do weather conditions affect traffic volume and travel time?
What is the impact of road construction activities on congestion levels?
Does higher parking usage contribute to increased traffic density?
How does public transport usage influence congestion?
What role do traffic signals play in traffic distribution?
How does traffic volume vary month by month?


7.Business Insights
Areas like Koramangala and M.G. Road show consistently higher traffic volume compared to other regions.
Roads such as Hebbal Flyover, Silk Board Junction, and Sarjapur Road are major congestion hotspots.
Rainy and foggy weather conditions significantly increase congestion and travel time.
Road construction activities directly lead to spikes in congestion levels.
Increased parking usage is associated with higher traffic density in busy areas.
Locations with better public transport usage show relatively controlled congestion.
Traffic signal status has a measurable effect on smooth traffic flow.
Monthly trend analysis helps identify peak traffic periods and seasonal patterns.
